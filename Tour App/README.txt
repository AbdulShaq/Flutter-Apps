Flutter Tour App:

	Simple Tour app of Chicago in Flutter that gives options for places to sleep, eat and see.
      Each tab offers 3 options showing the image of the location along with its name. When the user
      presses on the image of the location, the image will hero swoop into the next page where the address
      and average cost of the location along with a like button that will keep tracks of the nuumber of likes
      each location recieves and presents a snackbar notification when the user likes it.
