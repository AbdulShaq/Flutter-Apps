Flutter Movie App:

	Simple movie information multi-orientation mobile app that implements sliver widgets along with url launcher package for Flutter. 
 The app displays a Sliverlist that contains different movie categories along with a custom scroll view of clickable tiles that would display an image of a movie,
 the movie title, Directors, and stars of the movie. A short press on the tile will direct to selected movie’s poster, then another short press on the movie poster will bring the user to the movies ImDb webpage.
 A long press on the movie option will bring a pop-up menu to display 3 options: 1. Option to go to movie’s ImDb page; 2.Option to go to movie's Wikipedia page; 
 3.Option for more information on the movie such as runtime, Rotten Tomato Rating, ImDb Rating, etc.
